# Governance

This document explains how ASH is managed and how decisions are made.

---

## Ownership

ASH is developed and maintained by **3maem Co.**

The company provides:

- Core development
- Long-term maintenance
- Roadmap direction
- Security oversight
- Commercial sustainability

---

## Maintainers

Core decisions are made by the maintainers team.

Responsibilities include:

- Reviewing Pull Requests
- Managing releases
- Handling security issues
- Setting technical direction
- Ensuring code quality

Maintainers are appointed based on long-term contribution and trust.

---

## Community Contributions

We welcome contributions from everyone.

Community contributions are essential to ASH and directly influence the evolution of the open-source core.

Community members may:

- Submit issues
- Open Pull Requests
- Suggest improvements
- Participate in discussions

All contributions are reviewed fairly and respectfully.

---

## Decision Making

Decision flow:

1. Technical proposals discussed publicly
2. Maintainers review
3. Final decision by core team

Security and stability always take priority.

---

## Commercial Features

To ensure long-term sustainability:

- The core library remains open-source
- Advanced and enterprise features may be commercial
- Commercial roadmap decisions remain under company ownership

This allows ASH to stay free while funding continued development.

---

## Our Philosophy

We believe:

- Open collaboration builds better security
- Clear ownership prevents chaos
- Sustainable funding ensures longevity

ASH is open-source, but professionally maintained.
